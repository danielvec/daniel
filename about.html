About

Hi, I'm Daniel. I'm a self-taught developer with an analytical background. I love to work with data and solve problems.


I've spent the past ~5 years doing research and development work in the Pharmaceutical industry. What really drives me in my work is solving problems and learning new and better ways to accomplish tasks. As a data enthusiast, my programming journey began with tinkering around with Python and the Pandas module. In January of last year I began working through the Ruby on Rails path of The Odin Project, a project-focused curriculum, and I've thoroughly enjoyed it!
